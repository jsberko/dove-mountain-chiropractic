Meet Brian Schnurr DC

Dr. Schnurr, Born in Arizona, attended Central Michigan University in Mount Pleasant, Michigan. He earned his bachelors degree and went on to continue his education at Palmer College of Chiropractic Florida. While attending school he frequently volunteered at outreach clinics to provide chiropractic care for the underprivileged and economic hardship individuals and families. Dr. Schnurr has treated a wide variety of patients from young children to active retired patients. He currently practices in Tucson, Arizona utilizing the many chiropractic techniques and therapies he mastered at Palmer College of Chiropractic Florida that has brought such a high level of results to his happy patients.
